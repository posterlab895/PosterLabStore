Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering framed posters from Poster Lab Store.

How do I place an order?

Browse the shop, choose your favorite poster, select the size and frame option, then add it to your cart. When you're ready, go to the cart, enter your name, phone, address, and payment method, then click "Place order" to send the order via WhatsApp.

What sizes are available?

We offer four sizes: 20x30 cm, 30x40 cm, 40x50 cm, and 50x70 cm. Prices vary by size — larger sizes cost more. All sizes can be framed or unframed.

What frame options do you offer?

We offer three options: no frame (poster only), black frame, and white frame. Frames add EGP 120 to the price. All frames are high-quality and ready to hang.

Can I upload my own design?

Yes! Use the "Upload Your Custom Design" product to upload any photo or design. We'll print it as a high-quality framed poster. You can upload JPG, PNG, or any common image format.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept VF-Cash and Instapay. Payment is made before we start printing your order. You'll receive the payment details after placing your order.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery typically takes 3–7 business days depending on your location. We deliver anywhere in Egypt. A delivery fee of EGP 35 is added to each order.

Can I cancel or change my order?

You can cancel or modify your order within 2 hours of placing it. After that, production may have already started. Contact us on WhatsApp to make changes.

Do you offer refunds or exchanges?

Yes. If your poster arrives damaged or defective, we will replace it free of charge. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery with a photo of the issue.

How do I contact you?

You can reach us directly on WhatsApp by clicking the WhatsApp icon button at the bottom of any page. We typically respond within a few hours during business days.
